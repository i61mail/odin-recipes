RFISSA

Description

rfissa is one of the most famous dish in morocco, at thursday the "rfissa" u can smell it outside the houses according to moroccan traditions.

ingredients

  • chicken
  • lentils
  • msemen

steps

  1. take msemen and cut it intp pieces
  2. You should cook the lentils with the chicken to infuse the flavour
  3. finally put the cuted msemen in a plate and fill it up with the lentils and then the chicken
  4. "bonne appetit"
